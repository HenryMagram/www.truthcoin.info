What is Truthcoin?

Contracts as Smart as Whatever's Reading Them

Truthcoin attempts to create a trustless oracle, which reliably absorbs accurate data into a blockchain for use in Prediction Markets. These markets have many applications (which include superior alternatives to Lighthouse, Counterparty/Medici, BitsharesX, and Ethereum) and have the potential to revolutionize the way information is created and spread.

If you've never even heard of Bitcoin before, try What is Truthcoin? (10 fun slides) to learn about what you've been missing this whole time.

Then hit the documents on the right, starting with: Why do this?

A "Free Lunch" for Bitcoin

Planned to be a sidechain of Bitcoin. This means that you can use Bitcoin to take advantage of Truthcoin's features (to create markets and trade in them). A separate coin is used solely to power the trustless oracle (to eliciting honest reports about market-outcomes).

The sidechains graphic from http://blockstream.com.

The Goal

Speaking technically, this project aims to get external data into the blockchain reliably, and use that data efficiently and conservatively.

However, unlike in other blockchain projects, the ultimate purpose(s) is NOT to establish a new payment system (or delude the innocent with get-rich-quick nonsense), but instead to revolutionize the way information is created and spread, by...

  • ...providing a novel way to efficiently fund public goods, without taxation or coercion...
  • ...cutting out all Wall St. / banking middlemen and replacing them with tech...
  • ...spearheading the campaign to make Bitcoin the world's internet currency...
  • ...and more!
