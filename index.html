What is Truthcoin?

You can do two things with money: spend it or invest it. Bitcoin was about spending, Truthcoin is about investing (and much, much more)!

Check out Truthcoin in Six Slides for an introduction, and What is Truthcoin? for a little more detail, then hit the documents on the right!

Basically, we're talking about:

  • Blockchain Prediction Markets
  • BitUSD (Betting on Dollars to make a CryptoDollar)
  • Smart Contracts
  • Within-Protocol Colored Coins
  • A Second Scientific Revolution
  • ...and more!
