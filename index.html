A Tasty Lunch

The goal of this project is to get external data into the blockchain reliably, and use that data efficiently and conservatively.

Basically, we're talking:

  • Blockchain Prediction Markets
  • BitUSD (Speculating on Dollars to produce a CryptoDollar)
  • Smart Contracts
  • Within-Protocol Colored Coins
  • Trustless Dominant Assurance Contracts
  • ...and more!

Try to imagine:

  • The network effect of Bitcoin...
  • ...the betting feeds of Counterparty without having to trust the feed-operator to broadcast honestly...
  • ...the programmable flexibility of Ethereum, with much less complexity...
  • ...the BitAssets of BitsharesX, but done right (no soviet-style price fixing, or fiat 'name only' assets, and no swapping out the tried-and-true security model with what someone had for breakfast today)...
  • ...definitely none of the network-effect-killing 100%-premine of NXT (what??)...
  • ...nothing whatsoever to do with Peercoin's proof of stake (because proof of stake is insecure and pointless, and Peercoin is consequentially proof-of-work anyway)...
  • ...the fun of Dogecoin (for me, anyway)...
  • ...none of the organizational awkwardness / Byzantine-fault-intolerance of Ripple...
  • ...nothing whatsoever to do with Litecoin, The Great BitcoinTalk Failure...
  • ...plus near-instant-speed share-trading with the ease of a single Bitcoin tx!
